Welcome, Survivor

Welcome to Ethos Heights

This survivor manual walks you through everything you need to know about Mythic Spellbook — which could, or could not, be a game. But that does not matter. Let's get to your survival.

Which starter deck you pick, what sort of adventure you go on, and who you meet along the way — it is all up to you. We have so much more to add to this world, so stay tuned: this guide will keep growing right alongside it.
